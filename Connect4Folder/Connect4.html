Connect 4

× How to play

Connect 4 is a game that allows you to experience the pleasure of a strategic game against a friend. It works by dropping a red or yellow token into the grid. The goal of the game is to be the first to form a row of 4 tokens of your own color horizontally, vertically or diagonally. You can block your opponent's formation row.


To change the game mode (AI vs player or player vs player) you must select the game mode from the drop-down menu and click on the "Game on!" button. By default it is player vs player, and the player starts the game if it is player vs AI.
